The ABCDE rule of melanoma
A
Asymmetry
▶
One half doesn't match the other in shape or appearance.
Key indicator · Compare both halves along any axis
B
Border
▶
Edges are irregular, ragged, notched, or blurred.
Watch for · Scalloped, uneven, or fading edges
C
Color
▶
Uneven shades of brown, black, red, white, or blue.
Concern · More than 2 distinct colors or unusual areas
D
Diameter
▶
Larger than 6 mm — roughly the size of a pencil eraser.
Threshold · 6mm (smaller can still be dangerous)
E
Evolution
▶
Any change in size, shape, color, or new symptoms over weeks.
Action · Document changes monthly with photos
